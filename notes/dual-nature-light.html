Dual Nature Light

January 10, 2017

Light, such an interesting form of energy! Since an animal is born, and it opens eyes for the first time, this energy enters their eyes, and provides a massive information flow. Probably the most information they will ever get is from the medium of light.

Today I stumbled on an article for dual nature of light. It is a fascinatiing concept. So I wanted to see this for myself, so I tried to see what could be done to get this going , my solution was to use a hair as a windowing/splitting mechanism, on top of a laser pointer, and that worked like a charm !

So if you want to try this out for yourself, thats something you could do quite easily.

Now that I think of this, I think I have seen a similar kind of effect on bags filled with water, or if you turn a lense in a particular way. I am not sure if its the same interference mechanism that creates that type of behavior, but it looks very similar, just instead of a line, its more like a 2D projection. And there might be different starting conditions since here more complicated things like refraction and reflection are involved.
